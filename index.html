About me

Learn more about me

Allow me to introduce myself as a lover of the arts and knowledge. I am enthusiastic about books, chess, classic movies, classical music, computers, poetry, philosophy, and writing. This combination of interests allows me to have a broad and critical view of the world and the issues that surround me.

Regarding books, I am an unconditional fan of classic works, true crime, physics, horror, fiction, existentialists, etc. In chess, I see it not only as a strategy game, but also as an opportunity to challenge my logic and reasoning. As for classic films, I admire the immortality of these works, involving the image and the soundtrack in a subtle and impactful way. A good example of this are works such as Taxi Driver (1976) and Halloween (1978).

Classical music has a special place in my heart, as I have always been touched by its complexity and symphonic beauty. Finally, poetry, philosophy, and writing, which inspire me to reflect on existential questions and seek to understand the world and life in a deeper way. I think that writing is a good way to get to know yourself as well.

I would like to emphasize that these interests are not just hobbies for me, but constant sources of learning and personal growth.
